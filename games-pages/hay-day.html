حول Hay Day

Welcome to Hay Day, the fun farm simulator game! Build a farm, go fishing, raise animals, and explore the Valley. Farm with friends and decorate your own slice of country paradise.

Farming has never been easier! Grow crops like wheat and corn in this farm simulator, and even though it never rains, they never die. Harvest and replant seeds to multiply crops, then make goods to sell. Befriend animals like chickens, pigs, and cows as you expand and grow! Feed your animals to produce eggs, bacon, dairy, and more to trade with neighbors or fill truck orders for coins. This farming simulator is perfect for players who love animals, farming, and trade!

Become a farm tycoon with a thriving business. Expand with a Bakery, BBQ Grill or Sugar Mill to sell more goods. Grow your farm simulator empire like a true tycoon. Build a Sewing Machine and Loom to create cute outfits or a Cake Oven to bake delicious cakes. The opportunities are endless in this farming game!

Customize your farm and decorate with a wide variety of items. Decorate your farm simulator with unique touches that make farming fun. Build your dream farm step by step, raising animals, farming crops, and designing your land.

Trade and sell items in this farm simulator by truck or steamboat. Trade crops, fish, and fresh goods from your animals, and share resources to gain experience and coins. Become a successful farming tycoon with your own Roadside Shop. In this farm simulator, trading is key: trade, farm, build, fish, and decorate to rise as a tycoon!

Expand your farm simulator experience and play with friends. Join a neighborhood, or create your own with up to 30 players. Exchange tips and help each other create amazing farms! Play with friends in this farming simulator to build, trade, and fish together.
